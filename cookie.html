Cookie Policy for Moodify

Effective Date: 27/11/2024

Moodify ("we," "us," or "our") is committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring that your personal data is handled responsibly. This Cookie Policy explains how we use cookies and similar tracking technologies when you visit or interact with our platform.


What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files placed on your device (computer, tablet, or smartphone) by websites you visit. They are widely used to make websites work efficiently, improve user experiences, and provide website owners with useful information.


How We Use Cookies

Moodify uses cookies to:

  • Enhance User Experience: Save your preferences and optimize your browsing experience.
  • Analyze Platform Performance: Monitor and improve our website functionality using analytics.
  • Provide Personalized Content: Tailor content and features to your interests.
  • Ensure Security: Protect your data and our platform against unauthorized access or attacks.


Types of Cookies We Use

1. Essential Cookies

These cookies are necessary for our platform to function. They enable basic features like user authentication, secure browsing, and access to protected areas. Examples: Session cookies and Authentication cookies

2. Performance and Analytics Cookies

These cookies help us understand how users interact with our platform, such as pages visited, time spent on the site, and any issues encountered. This data is aggregated and anonymous. Examples: User behavior tracking

3. Functionality Cookies

These cookies allow Moodify to remember your preferences (e.g., language settings) and provide enhanced features. Examples: Remembering login details and Customizing content


Consent to Use Cookies

By continuing to use Moodify, you consent to our use of cookies as outlined in this policy. You can withdraw or modify your consent at any time by adjusting your cookie preferences in your browser or contacting us directly.


Updates to This Cookie Policy

We may update this policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or for legal, regulatory, or operational reasons. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.
