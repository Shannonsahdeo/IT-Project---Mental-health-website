Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to the most commonly asked questions about Moodify.

What is Moodify? +

Moodify is a platform designed to support mental health and wellness by providing tools, resources, and a supportive community to help users manage and improve their mental well-being.

Is Moodify free to use? +

Yes, Moodify offers a range of free resources and tools. We also have premium features available through a subscription for users who want a more personalized experience.

How does Moodify protect my data? +

We prioritize user privacy and security by implementing industry-standard encryption and data protection practices, ensuring your information is handled securely.

Can I track my mental health progress on Moodify? +

Absolutely! Moodify includes tools like journaling, mood tracking, and goal-setting to help you monitor your mental health journey and track improvements over time.

How can I contact Moodify for support? +

You can reach us through our email and Instagram.
